Mood board for ECI

Situation: the emphasis within ECI shifted to the online bussiness, and when we changed to a newer version of Intershop (7), we wanted a new design for the website.

Task: Finding the our target group and designing a moodboard.

Action: creating a client profile. Adepting design patterns for this client, keeping the branding guidelines in mind. I operated in conjunction with the designer of Belgium.

Result: a mood board for the sporty middle aged working-class wife (35 - 50) with two kids.
